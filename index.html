Biography

Greetings! I'm Ruoyan, a PhD researcher in Planetary Science at the University of Leicester, specializing in exploration of Jupiter’s infrared aurora via ground-based astronomical facilities and space telescopes.

I earned a BSc in Astrophysics at the University of California, Santa Cruz in the United States and a MSc in Astronomy at Leiden University in the Netherlands. Throughout my study, I have been awarded a University of California Regents Scholarship in 2013, a Leiden University Excellence Scholarship (LExS) in 2018, and a University of Leicester Postgraduate Research Support Grant in 2022, in recognition of outstanding academic merit. I am a fellow of the Royal Astronomical Society and I have been serving as a STEM ambassador of STEM Learning, supported by UK Research and Innovation.

During my spare time, I travel around and record the beauty of this fascinating planet earth as a freelance travel blogger. I'm a fan of basketball. Anyone is welcome to have a game with me!
