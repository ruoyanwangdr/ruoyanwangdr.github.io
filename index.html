Biography

Greetings! I'm Ruoyan, a PhD researcher in Planetary Science at the University of Leicester, specializing in exploration of Jupiter's infrared aurora via ground-based astronomical facilities and space telescopes.

I earned a BSc in Astrophysics at the University of California, Santa Cruz and a MSc in Astronomy at Leiden University. I am a fellow of the Royal Astronomical Society and a member of the American Geophysical Union. I have been serving as a STEM ambassador of STEM Learning, supported by UK Research and Innovation.

During my spare time, I travel around and record the beauty of this fascinating planet earth as a freelance travel blogger. I'm also a fan of basketball and badminton. Anyone is welcome to have a game with me!
